Privacy Policy

Welcome to Sans Simulator! Your privacy holds significant value to Sans Simulator. The privacy policy shows our procedures for handling data through website usage.

1. Information We Collect

The game system does not request personal details such as names or email addresses from users. The game records fundamental information including device kind and gameplay behaviors to optimize player experience.

2. How We Use Your Information

Business data collection occurs exclusively for game performance enhancement alongside bug fixes to deliver superior user experiences. The company keeps your data private and it never distributes your information to outside companies.

3. Cookies & Third-Party Services

Our website implements cookies as part of its function enhancement system. Game performance tracking occurs through third-party analytics tools which maintain no personal data collection capabilities.

4. Data Security

Reasonable precautions protect your data from harm. The platform does not offer absolute protection which matches the security of offline systems.

5. Changes to This Policy

The Privacy Policy might be modified through periodic updates. The page will feature all modifications to this policy.
