Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to us

The 2048 Game platform maintains total privacy protection of all personal data you share on our website. This website includes external links that direct users to other websites. Users are accountable for the content, privacy policies and practices maintained by external websites. Users should examine the policies of websites they visit from external sources.

Protection of Personal Information

We transfer personal information exclusively to employees and contractors together with affiliated organizations who need it to run data processing and deliver services. Data confidentiality standards exist for all these entities.
The personal information of users remains protected since 2048 Game does not distribute or rent this type of data to external entities. We will release personal data when we receive legal orders from courts or governments.

Aggregated Statistics

The site collects and shares non-personally recognized statistics regarding users' website activities. We do not reveal personal details in any such reports disclosed to the public.

Cookies

Our website employs cookies together with similar technological methods for enhancing user experience and saving personal preferences along with delivering appropriate advertisements. Cookies can be blocked from your browser however some website features will not function correctly under this restriction.
The ongoing website use implies your agreement to our cookie usage practices.

The Privacy Policy might receive periodic updates from time to time. The page offers users an opportunity to check for updates. The usage of this site by continuing visitors implies consent to the updated policy.
