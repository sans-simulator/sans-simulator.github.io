About Sans Simulator

People can now enjoy Sans Simulator which draws its inspiration from the Undertale franchise. The game allows both attacking Sans as a player or taking on the role of Sans by using fast moves alongside strategic plans to succeed. Although easy to learn Sans Simulator presents a demanding challenge that serious players want to overcome.

The developers made this game specifically for fans of Undertale who prefer intense battle experiences. The developers built Sans Simulator to give gamers of all experience levels a rewarding gameplay experience. Get prepared for the professional task ahead. The latest version of the game is available for testing.
